How Democratech Works Together

Democratech unites citizens, organizations, and government departments in a single, secure digital ecosystem. Votility Pro empowers organizations to run surveys, polls, and internal votes; Manifesto gives citizens the tools to debate, organize, and hold government accountable; Votility Gov provides government administrators with transparent and auditable oversight; and GOVAI leverages AI to make government operations smarter, faster, and more efficient. Together, these platforms create a connected democracy where every voice is heard, every decision is data-driven, and every process is transparent.
